About Us

The Diva Upgrades Drill team was established in 2011. Since we have done numerous amounts of community service in the North Seattle Community along with winning plenty of awards and trophy's. We strive for our girls to have fun along with a disciplined structure so they can be productive in their future with the community.

Our coaches are very patient and ready for any challenge. We our led by a past drill team member Renee Pierce who is well know in the North Seattle Community for her work. We encourage our members and parents to be active in all of our events. The Diva Upgrades are truly a family friendly team.

We practice at Bitterlake community center from 5pm-6pm. In May we change from 5pm-6pm to 4pm-6pm. Sign up fees $35.00 for Sign ups, then an additional $200.00 for entire uniform. An additional $50 for sweatsuit. Total price $285.00. Prices may increase depending on when you join. Prices may also decrease depending on fundraisers.
