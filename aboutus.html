About Us

The Diva Upgrades Drill team was established in 2011. Since that time, we've become well known in North Seattle through our history of community service, as well as our award winning team! By combining fun with service, Diva Upgrades have not only won awards for our performances, but we've watched girls blossom into responsible civically active young women.

Led by Renee Pierce, who participated in drill as a young woman, Diva Upgrades coaches are active role models whose patience helps foster the Divas' work ethic. The coaches help create a family-like environment for not only our girls, but their families as well.

Currently, we practice at Bitterlake Community Center Wednesdays and Fridays from 5pm-6pm. In preparation for performance season, in May we will extend our practices to 4pm-6pm. As of November 2016, sign up fees for new members are $35.00, and there is an additional $250.00 charge for the uniform and sweatsuit. Please contact us to confirm current pricing!
